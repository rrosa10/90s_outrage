Outrage in the 90s!

Mortal Kombat and similar games played a key role in the United States Senate hearings in 1993. These hearings, led by then-Senator Joe Lieberman, focused on the issue of violence in video games and led to discussions about the need for a video game rating system.Establishment of the ESRB: In response Senate hearings and the broader debate over video game content, the Entertainment Software Rating Board (ESRB) was created.
